Elementry School Coding Class

Our goal was to provide elementry aged students with a basic introduction to coding fundamentals over a 10 week long course. We utlized lessons in code.org to assist in this introduction to programming and debugging, then at the end of the course, we asked students to prepare a project to showcase their hard work and new coding knowlege!
